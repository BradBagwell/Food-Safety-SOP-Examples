# Food Safety SOP Portfolio

This repository contains sample food safety and operations documentation developed for small food manufacturing operations.

## Included Examples
- Sanitation Standard Operating Procedures (SSOPs)
- Preventive maintenance and calibration programs
- Employee hygiene and training SOPs
- Supplier approval and receiving procedures
- Product traceability and recall systems
- Production and quality logs

## Notes
- All documents are anonymized
- Content reflects real-world, inspection-ready systems
- Suitable for health department and audit review
